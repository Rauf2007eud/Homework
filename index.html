1 - Wheat tortilla recipe

Composition

  • Wheat four - 200 g;
  • Warm water - 70 ml;
  • Salt - 1/2 teaspoon;
  • Vegetable oil - 1 tablespoon;

Preparation

  • 1.Sift flour on the table with a slide, make a small depression in the middle.
  • 2.Dissolve salt in water.
  • 3.Pour water into the recess, add oil.
  • 4.Mix with careful movements, knead the dough.
  • 5.Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  • 6.From the dough, roll out thin tortillas about 25-30 cm in diameter.
  • 7.Fry in a dry hot frying pan for 10-15 seconds on each side.

2 - Braded chiken

Ingridients

  • 1.Chiken breast fillet - 300 g;
  • 2.For the marinade: spices, salt, olive or vegetable oil, wine vinegar;
  • 3.Corn flakes without sugar - 70 g;
  • 4.Flour - 70 g;
  • 5.Eggs - 2-3 pcs;
  • 6.Vegetable oil for deep-frying (2 liters) or frying in a frying pan (200 ml).

Preparation

  • 1.Rinse the fillet, cut into pieces 10cm long and 3-4 cm wide, put in a bowl.
  • 2.Add salt and spices , mix. Leave for 1,5-2 hours.
  • 3.Crush the cornflakes and pour them into a plate.
  • 4.Flour and beaten eggs with spices should also be poured into separate
  • 5.Dip fillets in eggs, roll in flour, dip in eggs, roll in flakes.
  • 6.Put it in s sacepan or put it on a preheated frying pan. Fry until golden brown.

3 - Hashbrown recipe

Ingridients

  • 1.Potatoes - 500 g;
  • 2.Salt, garlic and pepper to taste;
  • 3.Butter;
  • 4.2 frying pans.

Preparation

  • 1.Grate the potatoes on a coarse grater.
  • 2.Sqeueeze out excess water.
  • 3.Add salt, garlic passed through a press, and spices; mix thoroughfly;
  • 4.Melt the butter in a frying pan, lay out the potatoes with a thicknees of 1 layer, completely covering the bottom of the pan.
  • 5.Melt the butter in the second pan.
  • 6.When the Hashbrown is toasted, carefully turn the raw side over to the second frying pan. Repeat with the rest of the potatoes.
